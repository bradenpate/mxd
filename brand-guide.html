Brand Guide

Logo

Keyboards. Windows. Elements. Applications. Even floppy disks. Squares—simple, essential building blocks that, when combined, create extraordinary things. This foundational principle is the ethos behind the Mendix logo.

A simple, rounded square with our “MX” in the bottom left corner symbolizes our role in this composition. We are not at the center but an integral part of the framework. The open space represents the limitless potential of our customers—the room for their transformative, business-changing, and life-enhancing ideas to take shape and become reality.

Primary Logo

The primary logo for Mendix uses the "A Siemens Business" tagline. This is the default logo version for most applications. At very small sizes, the tagline can be removed.

Wordmark

The wordmark can stand alone as long as the primary logo is somewhere else within the asset. We use this treatment in the website navigation where space is limited. This version of the logo is also the predominate versiion used in the online platform.

Brandmark

The Solo Frame with MX is used for special moments and within the product. At smaller scales the MX is adjusted to take up the center width of the frame.
